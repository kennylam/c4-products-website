## Overview

Prioritizing the most appropriate information in a dashboard is paramount. As such, dashboards can be broken into multiple categories according to the intent by which their content is displayed.

Ultimately, the user is looking to report some form of narrative and it's up to the designer to aid in the construction of that narrative.
