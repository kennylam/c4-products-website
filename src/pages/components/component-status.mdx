### Description of component statuses:

1. **Work in progress** (`WIP`)
   - Such components are actively being worked on by CD&AI designers
   - Have not yet been submitted to Carbon
   - Are listed on **this website** (under the **Work in progress** section)
2. **CD&AI drafted component** (`CD&AI`)
   - Such components have been produced by CD&AI designers and are deemed by them to be ready for submission to Carbon
   - But have not yet been reviewed or accepted by the Carbon team
   - Are listed on **this website** (under the **Components** section)
3. **Available in Carbon as an "experimental" component** (`Carbon-exp`)
   - Such components have been successfully submitted to Carbon
   - Are listed on the **[Carbon website](https://www.carbondesignsystem.com/)** (under their **Experimental** section)
   - Are also listed on this site (with links provided to the Carbon entry)
4. **Available in Carbon as a fully-supported, stable component** (`Carbon`)
   - Such components have been promoted from "experimental" to fully-supported, stable Carbon components
   - Are listed on the **[Carbon website](https://www.carbondesignsystem.com/)** (under their **Components** section)
   - Are also listed on this site (with links provided to the Carbon entry)
