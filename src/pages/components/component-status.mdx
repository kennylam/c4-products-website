### Description of component statuses:

1. **Work in progress**
   - Such components are actively being worked on by Cloud & Cognitive designers
   - Have not yet been submitted to Carbon
   - Are listed on **this website** under the **Work in progress** section
2. **C&C**
   - Such components have been produced by Cloud & Cognitive designers and are deemed by them to be ready for submission to Carbon
   - But have not yet been reviewed or accepted by the Carbon team
   - Are listed on **this website** under the **Components** section
3. **Carbon (community)**
   - Such components have been successfully submitted to Carbon
   - These components are listed on the **Carbon website** under their [Community](https://www.carbondesignsystem.com/community/components) section
   - Full detatils about these components is provided on **this website**
4. **Carbon (stable)**
   - Such components are now fully-supported, stable Carbon components
   - These components now live on the **Carbon website** under the [Components](https://www.carbondesignsystem.com/components/overview) section
