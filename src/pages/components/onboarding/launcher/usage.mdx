Launchers are used to initialize an action. Launchers can be styled in various ways to express intent and set expectations for what action will occur when the user interacts with it.

</PageDescription>

## Overview

Launchers are action-oriented elements, or buttons, that can trigger a variety of paths once clicked. Examples include an footer tag that initiates a smart walk-thru tours, a button linking to a resource, or a pulsing beacon that activates a smartTip.
