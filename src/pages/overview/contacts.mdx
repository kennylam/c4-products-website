<PageDescription>

The people involved in developing the guidelines, components, and patterns shown on this site.

</PageDescription>

The following group is involved in assessing what is available in the Carbon Design "ecosystem,"
the needs of our products, and where any gaps might exist. Where gaps are identified, this group works to
provide necessary guidelines, components, and patterns and tracks adoption. The **Carbon for IBM Products PAL**
is the single place to view guidance for all assets across IBM Software. The PAL is maintained by **two guilds**,
the Design System Adoption Guild (**DSAG**) and the Design System Implementation Team (**DSIT**).

**Design System Adoption Guild**

Each member of the Design System Adoption Guild plays an active role in leading their areaâ€™s adoption, governance, and requirements vetting of the Carbon Design System to ensure consistent and predictable user experiences.

**Design System Implementation Team**

The Design System Implementation Team drives and promotes the implementation of the Carbon Design System across IBM Software. They are ultimately responsible for fostering IBM's brand integrity, design quality, prioritization, and executing strategic initiatives within their business unit.

_Together both guilds:_

- Carefully critiques and manages all guidance that is published on the Carbon for IBM Products PAL.
- Collaborates on various design initiatives across all of IBM Software's business units.
- Ensures awareness, use, and reuse of all available solutions for teams building IBM products within the IBM Software portfolio.
