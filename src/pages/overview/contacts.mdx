The people involved in developing the guidelines, components, and patterns shown on this site.

</PageDescription>

The following group is involved in assessing what is available in the Carbon Design System, 
the needs of our products, and where any gaps might exist. Where gaps are identified, this group works to 
provide necessary guidelines, components, and patterns, submits relevant ones up to Carbon and tracks adoption.
