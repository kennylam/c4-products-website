Some useful content-related resources you might like to check out.

### IBM wide resources
- [Carbon Design System](https://www.carbondesignsystem.com/) — for everything Carbon-related.
- [IBM Content Design Guild Slack channel](https://ibm-studios.slack.com/archives/CAKNY6LSV) — a 
good place to seek and share content-related questions, suggestions, and best practices.
- [IBM Content](https://w3.ibm.com/standards/ibm-content/) — for content types and standards.
- [Tech Content @ IBM site](https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/tech-content-at-ibm) — for 
information about different content roles across IBM, content collaboration models, and much more.
- [IBM ProductPages](https://w3.ibm.com/systems/productpages/) — like BluePages 
but for IBM products, so useful for checking the official name of any IBM offering.
- [IBM Terminology database](https://w3.ibm.com/standards/terminology/cgi-bin/lookup.pl) — look up any 
terms not covered in this content guide. 
- Alternatively, you can access this same guidance from within Slack by using the handy **Termbot** app (just search for "Termbot" in the Slack search field).
- [IBM Style](https://www-03preprod.ibm.com/support/knowledgecenter/ibm_style/about.html) — 
Comprehensive ID resource covering all aspects of technical writing.
- [IBM Accessibility > Content design](https://www.ibm.com/able/toolkit/design/content/text-equivalents/) — 
for accessibility guidance relating to content.

### IBM Cloud & Cognitive Design resources
- [Cloud and Cognitive Design website](https://pages.github.ibm.com/cdai-design/) — information and resources for 
designers working in the Cloud and Cognitive Design org.
- [Cloud and Cognitive Design > Content design](https://pages.github.ibm.com/cdai-design/practices/content-design/overview) — 
for information about the content design practice and team working within Cloud and Cognitive Design.


### Recommended books and podcasts
- [Content Design](https://contentdesign.london/store/the-content-design-book/) — book by Sarah Richards
- [Content Strategy for the Web](https://www.contentstrategy.com/content-strategy-for-the-web) — book 
by Kristina Halvorson and Melissa Rach
- [The Content Strategy](https://www.contentstrategy.com/podcast) — podcast
- [Writers of Silicon Valley](https://www.writersofsiliconvalley.com/) — podcast
