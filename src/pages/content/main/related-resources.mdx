### Other IBM writing related resources
- [IBM Style](https://ibmdocs-test.mybluemix.net/docs/en/ibm-style) — comprehensive ID resource covering all aspects of technical writing.
- [IBM Content](https://w3.ibm.com/standards/ibm-content/) — for content types and standards.
- [Tech Content @ IBM site](https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/tech-content-at-ibm) — for information about different content roles across IBM, content collaboration models, and more.
- [IBM Terminology database](https://w3.ibm.com/standards/terminology/cgi-bin/lookup.pl) — look up any terms not covered in this content guide.
  - Alternatively, you can access this same guidance from within Slack by using the handy **Termbot** app (just search for "Termbot" in the Slack search field).
- [Glossary terms for IBM Cloud](https://cloud.ibm.com/docs/overview?topic=overview-glossary) — glossary of terms used on IBM Cloud.




### Recommended books, websites, and thought leaders
There are obviously many more UX writing books, websites, and thought leaders that could be mentioned, 
but here are a few top recommendations to get you started.

#### Books
- _Strategic Writing for UX_ by Torrey Podmajersky (available to IBMers on <a className="noLinkPadding" href="https://learning.oreilly.com/library/view/strategic-writing-for/9781492049388/cover.xhtml">O’Reilly</a>)
- _Writing is Designing_ by Michael J. Metts and Andy Welfle
- _Content Design_ by Sarah Richards

#### Websites
- [15-day UX writing challenge](https://dailyuxwriting.com/)
- [UX content collective](https://uxcontent.com/)
- [Working in content](https://workingincontent.com/)

#### Thought leaders who publish on Medium
- [Beth Dunn](https://medium.com/@bethdunn)
- [Jason Fox](https://jjfoxbox.medium.com/)
- [Erika Hall](https://medium.com/@mulegirl)
- [Rachel McConnell](https://rachel-mcconnell.medium.com/)
- [John Saito](https://medium.com/@jsaito)
- Medium articles tagged with [content design](https://medium.com/tag/content-design)
- Medium articles tagged with [UX writing](https://medium.com/tag/ux-writing)
