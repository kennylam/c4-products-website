Some useful content-related resources you might like to check out.

</PageDescription>

### IBM wide resources
- [Carbon Design System](https://www.carbondesignsystem.com/) - for everything Carbon-related.
- [IBM Content Design Guild Slack channel](https://ibm-studios.slack.com/archives/CAKNY6LSV) - a 
good place to seek and share content-related questions, suggestions, and best practices.
- [IBM Content](https://ibm-content.w3ibm.mybluemix.net/) - for content types and standards.
- [Tech Content @ IBM site](https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/tech-content-at-ibm) - for 
information about different content roles across IBM, content collaboration models, and much more.
- [IBM ProductPages](https://productpages.w3ibm.mybluemix.net/ProductPages/index.jsp) - like BluePages 
but for IBM products, so useful for checking the official name of any IBM offering.
- [IBM Terminology database](https://w3.ibm.com/standards/terminology/cgi-bin/lookup.pl) - 
look up any terms not covered in this voice and tone guide. (Alternatively, you can use the 
[Slack Termbot app](https://ibm-casdesign.slack.com/archives/D0100TWM5PZ).)
- [IBM Style](https://apps.na.collabserv.com/wikis/home?lang=en-us#!/wiki/W580a84a67242_4da6_81cf_69e3db4bdbfa) - 
Comprehensive ID resource covering all aspects of technical writing.

### CD&AI specific resources
- [CD&AI Design website](https://cdai-design.w3ibm.mybluemix.net/) - information and resources for 
designers working in the CD&AI Design org.
- [CD&AI Design > Content design](https://pages.github.ibm.com/cdai-design/content-design/) - a micro-site 
specifically about the content design practice and team working within CD&AI.


### Recommended books and podcasts
- [Content Design](https://contentdesign.london/book/) by Sarah Richards
- [Content Strategy for the Web](https://www.contentstrategy.com/content-strategy-for-the-web) 
by Kristina Halvorson and Melissa Rach
- [The Content Strategy](https://www.contentstrategy.com/podcast) (podcast)
- [Writers of Silicon Valley](https://www.writersofsiliconvalley.com/) (podcast)
