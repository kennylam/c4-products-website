### Description of pattern statuses:

1. **Work in progress** (`WIP`)
	- Such patterns are actively being worked on by CD&AI designers
	- Have not yet been submitted to Carbon
	- Are listed on **this website** (under the **Work in progress** section)
2. **CD&AI drafted pattern** (`CD&AI`)
    - Such patterns have been produced by CD&AI designers and are deemed by them to be ready for submission to Carbon
    - But have not yet been reviewed or accepted by the Carbon team
    - Are listed on **this website** (under the **Patterns** section)
3. **Available in Carbon as an "experimental" pattern** (`Carbon-exp`)
    - Such patterns have been successfully submitted to Carbon
    - Are listed on the **[Carbon website](https://www.carbondesignsystem.com/)** (under their **Experimental** section)
    - Are also listed on this site (with links provided to the Carbon entry)
4. **Available in Carbon as a fully-supported, stable pattern** (tag = `Carbon`)
    - Such patterns have been promoted from "experimental" to fully-supported, stable Carbon patterns
    - Are listed on the **[Carbon website](https://www.carbondesignsystem.com/)** (under their **Patterns** section)
    - Are also listed on this site (with links provided to the Carbon entry)
